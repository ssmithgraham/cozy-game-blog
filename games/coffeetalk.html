About

Coffee Talk is a simulation game where you work as a barista in an urban fantasy coffee shop. It was developed and published by Toge Productions and released for PC on Jan 29, 2020.

Features

Learn the stories of the inhabitants in an alternate version of Seattle, where various different fantasy races live together with humans. You can discover the branching storylines of your cafe's customers as you engage in simple yet satisfying gameplay of brewing up different drinks.

Other Info

This game's art style, soundtrack, and gameplay all ooze a feeling of calm and comfort. If you're looking for the vibe of a late night, cozy coffee shop this game will definitely deliver.

If slice-of-life stories aren't very engaging for you, you might not get too immersed in the stories presented in Coffee Talk. It's also a fairly short experience, so if you're looking for a game to sink a lot of hours in, you may want to look elsewhere.
