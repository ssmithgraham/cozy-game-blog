About

Stardew Valley was released February 26, 2016. Developed by a solo developer, it quickly became a popular reimagining of the farming simulator genre popularized by games like Harvest Moon.

Features

Stardew Valley gives you the tools to transform an old farm plot into a thriving way of life!

  • Buy all kinds of seeds from the local general store to plant in your farm!
  • Build different structures on your land through the local carpenter.
  • The game emphasizes socializing with the townsfolk in Pelican Town, where you are able to get to know their stories, raise their friendship level, and maybe even get married.
  • You can also take a break from the farm life to explore the sprawling network of caves, where you can fight monsters and collect gemstones.

There's lots of secrets to discover in Stardew Valley!

Other Info

This game is perfect for getting comfy and spending an evening building up your farm! Pelican Town gives off a lovely, comforting atmosphere and the soundtrack is sure to provide a warm, pleasant mood while you play. The gameplay loop is very satisfying as you continuously make progress on your farm, and you should feel free to play at your own pace.

Some may find the freedom Stardew Valley gives the player a bit overwhelming, so if you prefer a clear sense of direction you might want to consider another chill game recommendation.
