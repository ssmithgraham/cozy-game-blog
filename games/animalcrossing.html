About

Animal Crossing New Horizons is the 5th and newest entry in the Animal Crossing series. It released on March 20, 2020 for Nintendo Switch. It is a life simulation game that works in real time.

Features

Animal Crossing places you in a town full of cute animal villagers with different personalities. You're encouraged to befriend your animal villagers and help develop the town. In New Horizons, you have complete customization over your town with access to terraform tools and the ability to place furniture all over your island. You can also spend you time planting flowers, digging up fossils, fishing, or catching bugs!

Other Info

Animal Crossing New Horizons is a great game to chill out to. You have total control over your own play style whether you want to maximize your bell(money) output, collect everything in the game, or just put in a few minutes in your freetime to chat to your villagers. Its a super soothing experience to take a walk down the beach or listen to the wind rustle the leaves of your very own apple orchard. Definitely take some time to lounge in your coziest chair with your favorite beverage and get to know the world of Animal Crossing!

If you find yourself running out of things to do in New Horizons, I would definitely recommend checking out the previous game in the series, New Leaf! Animal Crossing New Leaf allows a greater level of progression for your town with several buildings, upgrades, and NPCs to meet not available in New Horizons. I would even suggest checking City Folk out if you want even more Animal Crossing content!
