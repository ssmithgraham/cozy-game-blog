About

Slime Rancher is an action adventure life simulation game developed and published by Monomi Park. It released for PC on Aug 1, 2017 and is available on all major consoles.

Features

In this charming slimy game, you obtain a ranch full of opportunities.

  • Set out across the Far Far Range to collect different kinds of slime creatures that inhabit the land to house in your ranch.
  • Earn money to upgrade your tools and your ranch. You can use plots across your ranch to grow crops and raise chickadoos, which are used to feed your slimes!
  • In your adventures, you'll also uncover Beatrix's story and the secrets of the Far Far Range.

Try mixing and matching different slimes to find all the combinations!

Other Info

This game has an adorable atmosphere with all the different kinds of slimes available. You can forge your own path and develop your ranch how you want to! The art style is super charming and the gameplay loop is very fun. I loved unlocking different parts of my ranch and exploring the many areas the game has to offer. Be sure to keep an eye out for the sequel, Slime Rancher 2!

This game can get a little hectic compared to some of the other recommended games, so if you want a truly zen, no stress experience, this might be a little more involved with its elements of resource management. However, there's something undeniably relaxing about rounding up some slimes and harvesting some carrots, I'd check it out!
